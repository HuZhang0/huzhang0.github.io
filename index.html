About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Ningbo University, supervised by Prof. Zhufei Chu. I received my B.S. degree from Anhui Agricultural University (2022) and my M.S. degree from Ningbo University (2025). My research focuses on logic synthesis and combinational equivalence checking (CEC), with particular interest in structural choices, technology mapping, and SAT-based verification. I am also an active contributor to the open-source logic synthesis framework ALSO.
