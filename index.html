Zhang Hu

Ph.D. Student, Ningbo University

Zhang Hu is currently a Ph.D. student at Ningbo University, supervised by Prof. Zhufei Chu. He received his B.S. degree from Anhui Agricultural University and his M.S. degree from Ningbo University. His research interests focus on logic synthesis and combinational equivalence checking (CEC).
